Fun Facts

#Fact 1

The two most economically important varieties of coffee plant are the Arabica and the Robusta .

#Fact 2

Arabica beans consist of 0.8–1.4% caffeine whereas Robusta beans consist of 1.7–4% caffeine.

#Fact 3

~60% of the coffee produced worldwide is Arabica and ~40% is Robusta .
Find our more detail comparison check out Overview.

#Fact 4

Coffee Beans Criteria

Aroma
Fleeting notes are reflected most clearly before the coffee is actually tasted.

Flavor
It is a synthesis of all acidity, aroma and body.

After taste
It is the resonant silence at the end of the piece.

Acidity
It should be distinguished from sour or astringent.

Body
It is the sense of heaviness, tactile richness, or thickness.

Balance
It describes a coffee that does not localize at any one point on the palate.
